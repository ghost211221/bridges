HTML Structure

Construction is built with Clean and Smart Design. Construction has a full responsive layout, look very good on all devices (desktops, tablets, and phones) which you can easily adjust the rows and columns. Moreover, you can dispose of elements on page to build your own homepage. Below is the structure of Home pages:

<head>
</head>
<body>

    <!-- page -->

    	<!-- Content go here... -->

</body>

Contact Form

To edit the contact form to send mails to your email box, you can look for the contact-process.php. You need to edit yourname@yourdomain.com to your email.

contact-process.php ( includes/contact/contact-process.php )


define("WEBMASTER_EMAIL", 'your-email@domain.com');
